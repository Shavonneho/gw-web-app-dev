Tips

Meet your dog's basic needs

  • Regularly grooming helps your dog keep clean, healthy, and pest-free. Brush their fur, trim, and their nails, and keep an eye out for ticks and fleas. Few suggestions on grooming tools down below.

  • Give your dog plenty of opportunities for daily exercise and mental simulation by walking, running, or providing them with puzzle toys. Paw-some dog toys links are down below.

  • Provide your pup with a quiet, warm area to sleep and unwind-- a comfortable, clean safe place off the floor and out of the way.

  • Keep your pet's registrations and licensing up-to-date. Ensure that their dog tag is securely attached to their collar.

  • Use a leash while in public to help ensure the safety of your dog and others.

  • Head to the vet for annual checkups, to stay on top of your dog's health and keep ahead of health concerns.

  • Take care of your dog's teeth. To keep your dog's pearly whites sparkling, brush your dog's teeth or take your dog for a dental cleaning at the vet.

  • Avoid overfeeding your pet and provide them with a quality diet to help prevent obesity and other health concerns.

  • Make sure your dog has easy access to fresh water.
